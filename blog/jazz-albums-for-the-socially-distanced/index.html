Jazz Albums for the Socially Distanced
Mar 16, 2020
5 minutes read

I like jazz music - when I was a kid I was obsessed with Duke Ellington. Recently I’ve been thinking about trying to get back in to jazz. I recently picked up a headphone amp and DAC specifically for focused music listening, and jazz seems like an ideal genre to dive in to. I recently asked on Twitter for some recommendations for albums:

Hey twitter! What are your favorite 5 Jazz albums?

I want to expand my horizons beyond just re-listening to “Kind of Blue” :P

— cron mom (@sophaskins) March 13, 2020

I got a tremendous response - at this time, I have 135 album recommendations! Since I suspect many of you are also cooped up to “socially distance” to combat COVID-19, I figured I’d format the recommendations and share them.

Top Artists

Artists for whom I got at least 5 recommendations were (in descending order):

  • John Coltrane
  • Miles Davis
  • Dave Brubeck
  • Charles Mingus
  • Thelonious Monk
